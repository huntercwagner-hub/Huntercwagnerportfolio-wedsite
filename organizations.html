Professional Organizations & Leadership

Throughout my time at the University of West Florida, I’ve actively participated in professional engineering organizations that promote collaboration, leadership, and career development within the STEM community. My involvement has provided valuable experience in networking, event organization, and professional outreach.

Society of American Military Engineers (SAME)

Position: Chapter President

Term: January 2024 – Present

As founding president of the UWF chapter, I led the development of a new student branch to connect students with defense, civil, and environmental engineering professionals. Responsibilities included coordinating guest speakers, managing partnerships with local SAME posts, and leading volunteer and outreach initiatives for campus-wide events.

American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics (AIAA)

Position: Officer of Professional Relations

Term: Summer 2024 – Summer 2025

Served as the primary liaison between students and industry professionals, organizing speaker sessions, company tours, and resume workshops. Contributed to the AIAA Design Build Fly team and helped strengthen collaboration between AIAA and UWF’s mechanical engineering department.

American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME)

Member

Participated in chapter meetings, technical workshops, and student design competitions to enhance professional growth and stay connected with mechanical engineering advancements and networking opportunities.
